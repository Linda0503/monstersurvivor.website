About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you face relentless waves of monsters. Upgrade your hero, unlock powerful abilities, and test your reflexes as you strive to survive as long as possible. Perfect for fans of roguelike and bullet-hell games!

  • Free to play directly in your browser
  • No downloads or registration required
  • Optimized for both PC and mobile devices
  • Regular updates and new challenges

How to Play

  1. Use your keyboard or touchscreen to control your hero.
  2. Defeat monsters and collect upgrades.
  3. Survive as long as you can to achieve a high score!
